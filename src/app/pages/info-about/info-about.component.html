About HeroQuest Cards

Welcome to HeroQuest Cards! We created this web for all the HQ community. Our main objective is to create the biggest repository of HQ cards.

Whether you need new heroes, artifacts, or epic monsters, this is the place to share your ideas and discover what others have created. With a growing collection of fan-made content from around the world, there is always something new to explore.

Join us and help shape the biggest repository of HQ cards in the ever-expanding world of HeroQuest!

Features - What can you do here?

Create your Custom Cards

Design and generate unique HeroQuest cards with our intuitive card builder. Add custom artwork, stats, and special abilities to your heroes, monsters, spells, equipment, treasures and artifacts.

Explore the biggest Card Repository

Browse through an extensive collection of custom cards created by the community. Find inspiration and discover new ways to enhance your HeroQuest adventures.

Create and share your Collections

Organize and save your favorite cards into custom collections. Build thematic sets and share them with other HeroQuest fans all around the world.
