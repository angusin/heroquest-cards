Why is HeroQuest Cards Free?

We are passionate about growing a vibrant community where HeroQuest fans can share their creations and enhance their gameplay experience. For now, we're focused on making the platform as robust as possible, and we believe offering everything for free is the best way to build a strong community.

If you find our platform helpful, consider supporting us through Ko-fi. Your support helps us maintain and improve the platform for the entire HeroQuest community.

Will My Data Be Affected?

Whether you're using the free or premium version, your data (such as custom cards) will remain the same. We'll make sure that all users have a seamless transition to any future premium offerings, and your creations will always be accessible to you.
